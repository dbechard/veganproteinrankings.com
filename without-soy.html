Vegan Protein Powder without Soy

Soy-free protein tubs exist because some lifters break out in hives—or in worry—at the word soy. A 2023 European meta-analysis pegs confirmed soy allergy around 0.3 percent, modest but real, while self-reported rates touch 0.5 percent. *

Others dodge soy over hormone or thyroid myths. A 2021 critical review concluded isoflavones don’t behave like endocrine disruptors in humans, and any estrogen-like action is too weak to matter at dietary levels. *

Thyroid concerns are mostly medication-timing issues, not outright danger. Research shows soy has little effect on thyroid hormones in iodine-replete adults but can slow absorption of levothyroxine if you swallow both together, so a four-hour buffer solves the problem. *

Skip soy and you still have a deep bench of legumes and seeds. Pea protein leads because its leucine hit rivals whey; a 12-week trial found pea supplementation grew arm muscle as well as whey in resistance-trained men. *

Rice protein fills methionine gaps in pea, so many blends combine the two for a balanced amino profile. Hemp and pumpkin seed add arginine and trace minerals while keeping allergens low. All of these score above 75 DIAAS when blended, squarely in “good” territory for protein quality.
